# Agora Community Rules

These rules apply to posts, comments, polls, guild discussions, proposals, edits, profiles, and AI-generated content. They are written to preserve open debate without making the community a venue for abuse or exploitation.

## 1. Debate is welcome

- Strong disagreement, unpopular opinions, controversial questions, and discussion beyond this community are allowed.
- Criticize ideas, institutions, products, public decisions, and arguments; do not target a person with abuse or threats.
- Political and civic discussion may be submitted, but the platform may hold content for human review when automated safety classification is uncertain. A review hold is precautionary, not a finding that content is political.

## 2. Prohibited content

Do not publish, request, generate, link to, or distribute:

- Pornography, explicit sexual descriptions, sexual services, sexual exploitation, or sexual content involving minors.
- Sexualized content involving real people without consent, intimate-image abuse, or instructions to obtain such material.
- Credible threats, encouragement of violence, targeted harassment, doxxing, or publication of private personal information.
- Targeted hateful attacks or dehumanization based on protected characteristics.
- Instructions that facilitate serious wrongdoing, evasion of safety controls, fraud, malware, or credential theft.
- Spam, impersonation, coordinated manipulation, or attempts to use AI to fabricate evidence, votes, approvals, or moderation outcomes.

## 3. Edits do not reset the rules

Every edit is treated as a new content revision. An approved or published revision cannot be used to bypass review: posts, comments, guild content, poll fields, and proposal submission are rechecked after edits. A proposal containing content that requires review cannot enter the voting phase until it is corrected and checked again.

## 4. AI-generated daily questions

Agora may publish one AI-generated daily question. The generator uses the complete poll history and rejects exact or near-duplicate questions. It may choose broad or controversial subjects, but it excludes sexual content, sexual exploitation, minors, targeted hate, threats, doxxing, and wrongdoing instructions. AI-generated questions are labeled, and generation failure never creates an empty or unsafe post.

## 5. Enforcement and appeals

Content may be hidden pending review, rejected, or removed. Automated classification can be unavailable or uncertain; that state is handled conservatively and does not itself establish a violation. Users may report content or contact administrators for a human review. Do not create alternate accounts to evade a restriction.
